Defense Towers

There are different types of towers, some with adjustable targeting mode, some can shoot at multiple targets. A tower can also be upgraded: a boost upgrade improves a tower's statistics, whereas effect upgrade adds an effect. Only one upgrade can be applied per tower.

Singleshot Tower The cheapest tower you can get. Also the fastest.

Multishot Tower Weaker power, but will shoot all marked sparks within its range.

Circle Tower Will shoot projectiles in 8 directions. Slightly more powerful than multishot power, but also shorter range.

Pierce Tower A more expensive variant of the singleshot tower with almost twice its range. It is twice slower though.

Sniper Tower A long range tower with deadly power. Do watch your funds since this power is expensive.

Targeting mode

Singleshot, Pierce, and Sniper towers allow you to adjust its targeting mode:

  • Closest : the tower will target the closest marked spark.
  • First : the tower will target the oldest marked spark in its range (so, the one that first entered the range).
  • Last : the tower will target the most recent marked spark in its range (so, the one that last entered the range).
  • Weakest : the tower will target the weakest spark in its range.
  • Strongest : the tower will target the strongest spark in its range.
